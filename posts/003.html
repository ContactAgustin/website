Waterbearer Handcrafted Beard Oils & Maintenance

Published: September 12, 2025

I created these recipes as a set, which I call Waterbearer Handcrafted Beard Oils, and a schedule to follow at the end. They’re made for beard growth and maintenance, the scheduled routine includes use of a microneedle roller.

I provided a list of each oil with the amounts in ounces and milliliters for reference when purchasing. After the list is each recipe I've created, along with an easy name to remember it by. Please read and follow them accordingly. After you've prepped each oil, you'll find a short microneedle guide and schedule right after the recipes.

For this set, you'll need

6 glass dropper vials 4oz each
Labels
A small funnel
Microneedle Roller

Goal: Stimulate sparse areas, soothe skin after rolling.

Using the Microneedle Roller

Start small: Use a 0.25 mm roller
Move up if comfortable: Once your skin feels fine, you can try a 0.3–0.5 mm roller for better growth.
How to roll: Go up and down, side to side, and diagonally a few times each way.
After rolling: Apply Wild Mint Oil to soothe the skin and support hair growth.
Frequency: 1–2 times per week
